Frequently Asked Questions

  1. ✦

    Who are these sessions designed for?

    Ivana's sessions welcome anyone standing at a threshold — whether you are navigating a major life transition, seeking deeper self-understanding, or simply curious about the patterns written in your chart. No prior knowledge of astrology or tarot is required. The work meets you exactly where you are, with language that is clear, grounded, and deeply personal.

  2. ✦

    What preparation is needed before a session?

    For an astrology reading, you will need your date, place, and — most importantly — your exact time of birth. If you are unsure of your time of birth, check your birth certificate, as it typically records this detail. Even an approximate hour is helpful. For tarot sessions, simply arrive with an open question or an area of life you wish to illuminate. No special rituals or preparations are required beyond your honest intention.

  3. ✦

    Can astrology and tarot be combined in one session?

    Yes — and this is where Ivana's alchemy truly comes alive. When the natal chart and the tarot speak together, patterns emerge that neither discipline reveals alone. A combined reading weaves your celestial blueprint with the symbolic language of the cards, offering a layered, nuanced portrait of where you stand and where you are headed. This integrated approach is available as a dedicated experience.

  4. ✦

    What style of guidance can I expect?

    Ivana's approach is contemplative rather than prescriptive. You will not receive a list of instructions or predictions delivered as certainties. Instead, the session creates a reflective space where you are invited to recognise what you already sense, supported by symbolic frameworks that sharpen your intuition. The tone is warm, precise, and respectful — a dialogue between your inner knowing and the wisdom of the cosmos.

  5. ✦

    Are sessions available in Hebrew and English?

    Yes. Ivana works fluently in both Hebrew and English, and you are welcome to choose whichever language feels most natural to you. Many clients find that speaking in their mother tongue allows a deeper, more unguarded conversation — and that is precisely the quality of presence Ivana seeks to cultivate in every exchange.

  6. ✦

    What is the expected rhythm after a session?

    Following your session you will receive a written summary of the key themes discussed, so you can return to the insights at your own pace. There is no pressure to book immediately again — the work is designed to unfold over time. Many clients find that a single session opens a thread of reflection that continues for weeks. When you feel ready to go deeper, Ivana will be here.
